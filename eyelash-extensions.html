Eyelash Extensions

Eyelash extensions are used to enhance the length, curliness, fullness, and thickness of natural eyelashes.The lashes are applied one at a time using a specially formulated, semi-permanent glue that will not irritate the eye nor damage the natural lash. This process will take up to an hour and a half.
