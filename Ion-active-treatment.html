Ionactive Power Treatment

IonActive Power Treatment works with the skin's natural biology to yield enhanced benefits. The treatment takes about an hour and 20 minutes,the treatment utilizes ultrasonic and Led red/blue Light machine. They benifits include brighter, revitalized skin; fewer visible fine lines and wrinkles; fewer visible breakouts; improved skin texture and more balanced skin tone. One of the following concentrates will be selected by the esthetician to be used on your skin:


  • Retinol-for skin aging
  • Hyaluronic Acid-for dehydrated skin.
  • Oligopeptide-for hypperpigmentation
  • Niacinamide-for adult acne
